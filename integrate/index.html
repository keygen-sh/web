Concierge Integration

Integration done for you

You want the features and power of Keygen, but the work to integrate might seem a little daunting. It's tough to figure out what licensing model you should use, how you want to go about accepting payments, etc. We've got documentation to help you through that whole process, but what if it could all be done for you?

That's where our concierge integration service is here to help!

Initial integration (or switching an existing licensing system) can be painful, but working together with our team can make it much easier. You'll still need to be involved to provide explanations and review our work, but it's far easier than doing everything yourself, especially if you're not a developer.

Regardless of what you're selling, whether that's a desktop application, on-premise software, a WordPress plugin—whatever—we're here to help you be successful. We'll communicate with you every step of the way to make sure your goals are met.

How it works

Once you reach out to us, we will schedule an onboarding call with you to go over all the details of how Keygen works and what licensing model we think would work best for you and your business. During this call we will go over our integration process, next steps, answer any final questions, and set expectations of completion.

How to qualify

Consultants typically quote over $1,000 for this type of service, but we want you to be successful using Keygen, so we've decided to offer it at no additional charge! We offer concierge integration for all plans (yes—even Indie), regardless of size. So if you're just starting out, we're still here to help! Just tell us a little bit about your product and we'll start from there.

To get started we've just got a couple steps:

  1. Create your Keygen account and choose the plan that's best for you
  2. Add a payment method to your account via your dashboard
  3. Reach out over email and mention concierge integration
