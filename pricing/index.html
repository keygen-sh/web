FAQs

  • What is a "licensed user"?

    A "licensed user" is a licensee of your product, either through a user resource, or through a license resource if you're not using our user management features, that has been active within the last 90 days. We determine your "licensed user" count through your API resource consumption alone.

    For example, a user resource which has 3 associated license resources with it will count as a single licensed user, while 3 license resources without user associations will count as 3 individual licensed users.

  • What if I go over my resource limits?

    Account limits are "soft" limits for all plans Tier 1 and higher—meaning they are not strictly enforced. That is, we won't immediately throttle future requests to your account (outside of our standard rate limiting), or stop allowing new users from signing up for your product, or immediately stop licenses or machines from being validated or created if you go over your account limits.

    However, we will send you a polite email with our recommended upgrade path from your current plan. An account will be auto-upgraded if its plan's resource limits are exceeded for a sustained period of time after our attempted outreach.

  • How are requests counted?

    Requests are any API request made to our licensing and distribution services for your account. Both client- and server-side requests will be counted, regardless of authentication. Duplicate requests will also be counted.

  • Can I see how many requests I have left?

    No, not at this time. We may allow this to be viewed in the future. The daily request limit is implemented so that a single account does not make an excessive number of API requests, e.g. validating a license every 30 seconds for 5,000 users.

    Please note: these limits are purposefully high numbers, so don't worry about request overages unless we directly contact you about an issue. We will likely not contact you unless you've gone over your limit by an order of magnitude.

  • Can I upgrade/downgrade my plan?

    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at anytime from your account dashboard. You can even pause your subscription if you ever need to!

  • Can I cancel anytime?

    We hate to see you go, but yes, you can cancel your account at anytime from your account dashboard.

    Alternatively, you can also "pause" your account's subscription at anytime, which will allow you to pick back up where you left off at a later time.

  • Do you offer yearly billing?

    Yes! If you're interested in being billed yearly, reach out! We offer 2 months off your plan for any yearly billing contract on our standard plans.

  • What if I need a larger plan?

    No problem! Need to make more requests than even our biggest plan, or have custom data needs or other special requirements? Reach out and we'll get you set up with exactly what you need.
