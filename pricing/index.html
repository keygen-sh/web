FAQs

  • What if I go over my limits?

    Account limits are soft-limits and are not strictly enforced, i.e. we won't throttle future requests to your account (outside of our standard rate limiting), or stop allowing new users from signing up for your product, or immediately stop licenses or machines from being validated or created if you go over your account limits. We will however send you a polite email with our recommended upgrade path from your current plan.

  • Can I upgrade/downgrade my plan?

    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at anytime from your account dashboard. You can even pause your subscription if you ever need to!

  • Can I cancel anytime?

    Absolutely! You can cancel your account at anytime from your account dashboard.

  • Can I export my data?

    Yes, we do offer data export services, but at this time it is a manual task. Reach out to us anytime with data export requests.

  • Do you offer yearly billing?

    Yes! If you're interested in being billed yearly, reach out! We offer 1 month off your plan for any yearly billing contract on our standard plans.

  • What if I need a larger plan?

    No problem! Need to make more requests than even our biggest plan, or have custom data needs or other special requirements? Reach out and we'll get you set up with exactly what you need.

  • Do you offer on-premise API plans?

    Yes. We're very interested in talking with companies that would rather host our software licensing and distribution servers in-house. Get in touch.
